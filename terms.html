1. What ZTR Is and Is Not

Zorthex Temporal Registry (ZTR) is a verification timestamping service. It creates contemporaneous records — dated, tamper-evident evidence that a human verification step was performed on a document at a specific time.

ZTR does not:

  • Verify the accuracy or completeness of document content
  • Detect errors, hallucinations, or fabricated citations in documents
  • Provide legal, regulatory, or compliance advice
  • Guarantee that any professional duty was fulfilled
  • Store, access, or transmit the original document text
  • Replace professional judgment

A ZTR receipt proves that verification occurred at a specific time under a specific context. It does not prove that the verification was thorough, correct, or sufficient to meet any particular professional standard.
